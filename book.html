In Fratelli, Santo Versace tells the story of growing up with his younger brother Gianni and building a global fashion empire together. The book is both personal and historical: it speaks about family, ambition, creativity, and the challenges behind success.

Fratelli is a memoir by Santo Versace that offers a rare internal perspective on the formation of the Versace fashion house, framed through the intimate bond between two brothers: Gianni and Santo Versace. Rather than a fashion biography in the conventional sense, the book functions as a document of cultural entrepreneurship, revealing how familial structures, regional identity (Southern Italy), and post-war economic conditions shaped one of the most influential luxury brands of the late 20th century.
